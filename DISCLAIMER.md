### Risk Mitigation

If you choose to use this Software despite the risks, the following features are designed to reduce (but NOT eliminate) detection risk:

| Feature | What It Does | Risk Reduction |
|---------|-------------|----------------|
| `activity_simulation` | Simulates human browsing | Moderate |
| `rate_limiter` | Detects ban signals, auto-throttles | Moderate |
| `scheduling` limits | Caps daily applications | Moderate |
| `fingerprint` rotation | Varies browser fingerprint | Low-Moderate |
| Human-like delays | Random jitter between actions | Low |
| `undetected-chromedriver` | Bypasses basic bot detection | Low |

**No combination of these features guarantees your account will not be flagged.** LinkedIn continuously updates their detection systems.

### Recommended Precautions

If you proceed, consider:

1. **Use a secondary LinkedIn account** — not your primary professional account
2. **Set conservative limits** — `max_applies_per_day: 20` or lower
3. **Enable activity simulation** — makes the account look more natural
4. **Don't run 24/7** — set `active_hours_start` and `active_hours_end` to reasonable business hours
5. **Monitor for warnings** — check logs for rate limiting signals
6. **Use `browser.user_data_dir`** — persistent cookies reduce re-login detection
7. **Don't combine with manual LinkedIn activity** on the same account simultaneously

### Legal Considerations

Depending on your jurisdiction, automated access to websites may implicate:

- **Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA)** — United States
- **Computer Misuse Act 1990** — United Kingdom
- **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)** — European Union (if scraping personal data)
- **Similar computer crime statutes** in other jurisdictions

The legal landscape around web scraping and automation is evolving. Notable cases:

- *hiQ Labs v. LinkedIn (2022)* — US court ruled scraping **public** data is not a CFAA violation, but this does not cover accessing data behind a login wall.
- LinkedIn's Terms of Service constitute a **contractual agreement** — violating them may expose you to breach of contract claims regardless of criminal law.

**Consult a legal professional in your jurisdiction if you have concerns.**

### Data Security Warning

The Software handles sensitive data:

| Data Type | Where Stored | Risk |
|-----------|-------------|------|
| LinkedIn password | `config.yaml` (local) | File exposure |
| Email password (if IMAP enabled) | `config.yaml` (local) | File exposure |
| AI API keys | `config.yaml` (local) | Unauthorized API usage |
| Job application history | `data/state.db` (local) | Privacy |
| Recruiter names/URLs | `data/state.db` (local) | Privacy |
| Tailored resumes | `data/tailored_resumes/` (local) | Contains PII |

**Protect your `config.yaml` file.** It is gitignored by default but you must ensure it is not:
- Committed to version control
- Stored in shared/cloud directories without encryption
- Accessible to other users on a shared system
